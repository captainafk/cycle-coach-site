Cycle Coach — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

The short version: All your data stays on your phone. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your information. Ever.

What data does Cycle Coach store?

Cycle Coach stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Cycle start dates you enter
  • Your notification preferences (on/off, preferred time)
  • The date you last opened the app

This data is stored using your device's local storage and never leaves your phone.

What data do we collect?

None. Cycle Coach does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no server. There is no account. There is no analytics. There are no ads.

Third-party services

Cycle Coach does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have access to any data. Everything stays on your device.

Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings → Clear All Data within the app. This permanently removes all cycle data and preferences from your device. You can also uninstall the app, which removes all stored data.

Children's privacy

Cycle Coach is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page and the date above. Given that we collect no data, significant changes are unlikely.
